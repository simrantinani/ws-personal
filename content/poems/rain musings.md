There's something endlessly romantic about the rain.  
So capricious, so moody.  
A luring sweet patter,  
A thunderous wild rage.  
Charming up close,  
Enchanting from a distance.  
Arrives unexpected,  
Departs too soon,  
Leaving behind a trickle.
